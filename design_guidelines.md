# Utech Life Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Selected Approach:** Design System Approach using Material Design principles
**Justification:** As a B2B laboratory equipment company, Utech Life requires a professional, trustworthy interface that prioritizes information clarity and corporate credibility over visual flair.

## Core Design Elements

### A. Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Primary Blue: 220 85% 20% (deep professional blue for headers, CTAs)
- Light Blue: 220 50% 96% (subtle backgrounds, sections)

**Neutral Colors:**
- Dark Gray: 220 15% 15% (primary text)
- Medium Gray: 220 10% 45% (secondary text)
- Light Gray: 220 15% 95% (backgrounds, borders)
- Pure White: 0 0% 100% (card backgrounds, main content areas)

**Accent (minimal use):**
- Success Green: 140 65% 35% (contact buttons, success states only)

### B. Typography
**Primary Font:** Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, professional, excellent readability
**Font Hierarchy:**
- Headings: Inter Semi-Bold (600) - sizes 2xl to 4xl
- Body text: Inter Regular (400) - size base to lg
- Captions/labels: Inter Medium (500) - size sm

### C. Layout System
**Spacing Units:** Consistent use of Tailwind units 4, 6, 8, 12, 16
- Component padding: p-6 or p-8
- Section spacing: mb-12 or mb-16
- Element margins: m-4 or m-6
- Container max-width: max-w-6xl

### D. Component Library
**Navigation:** Clean horizontal nav with company logo, product categories dropdown
**Cards:** White backgrounds with subtle shadows (shadow-sm) for product showcases
**Forms:** Simple contact forms with outlined inputs, professional styling
**Buttons:** Primary blue filled buttons, minimal outline variants on images
**Data Display:** Clean product category grids, specification tables
**Headers:** Professional page headers with breadcrumbs for product sections

### E. Visual Approach
**Professional Corporate Style:**
- Clean layouts with generous whitespace
- Structured grid systems for product displays
- Subtle shadows and borders for depth
- Minimal animations - only smooth transitions
- Focus on readability and information hierarchy

## Images
**Hero Section:** Medium-height hero (not full viewport) showing modern laboratory environment or chromatography equipment
**Product Images:** Clean product photography on white backgrounds for equipment and consumables
**Industry Images:** Professional laboratory settings, scientists at work
**Placement:** Hero on homepage, product category headers, about us section background

**Button Treatment:** Outline buttons on images should have backdrop-blur backgrounds for readability while maintaining image visibility.
